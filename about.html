Wine Varietals

Wine Primary Flavors Aromas Food Pairings
Cabernet Sauvignon Blackcurrant, blackberry, cedar, and sometimes green bell pepper. Dark fruits, vanilla, and sometimes hints of tobacco. Grilled meats, hearty stews, and aged cheeses.
Chardonnay Apple, citrus, tropical fruits, and butter (for oaked versions). Vanilla, butter, and sometimes hints of oak. Creamy pasta dishes, roasted chicken, and seafood.
Merlot Plum, black cherry, raspberry, and chocolate. Red fruits, vanilla, and sometimes herbal notes. Roast pork, grilled lamb, and mushroom-based dishes.
Pinot Noir Red cherry, strawberry, and earthy notes. Floral, red fruits, and sometimes hints of spice. Roast chicken, salmon, and dishes with earthy flavors.
